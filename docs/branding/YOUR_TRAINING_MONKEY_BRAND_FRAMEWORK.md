## Brand Identity

### Brand Essence
**Your Training Monkey** is the intelligent training companion for trail runners who demand more than basic metrics. We transform complex physiological data into actionable insights that prevent injuries and optimize performance.

### Core Promise
**"Prevent Injuries ‚Äî Train Smarter"**

We reveal the training insights your Garmin can't show you through patent-pending divergence analysis.

### Brand Personality (The 5 Pillars)

1. **Intelligent Yet Approachable**
   - We simplify complex sports science without dumbing it down
   - Data-driven but never overwhelming
   - Expert guidance delivered conversationally

2. **Trail-Runner Authentic**
   - Built for trail runners, by trail runners
   - We count vert because we know it matters
   - We understand the unique demands of mountain training

3. **Proactive & Protective**
   - Early warning system, not post-mortem analysis
   - Injury prevention over performance chasing
   - Guardian of sustainable training

4. **Scientifically Rigorous**
   - Patent-pending technology
   - Evidence-based recommendations
   - Transparent methodology

5. **Empowering & Supportive**
   - Coaching tone, not commanding
   - Encourages informed decisions
   - Celebrates sustainable progress

### Target Audience

**Primary Persona: "Sarah the Smart Trail Runner"**
- Age: 30-45
- Experience: Intermediate to advanced trail runner
- Goals: Complete ultras without getting injured
- Pain Points: Previous injuries from overtraining, conflicting training advice, basic metrics that miss the full picture
- Tech Comfort: High - uses Garmin/Strava religiously
- Values: Data-driven decisions, injury prevention, long-term athletic development

**Secondary Personas:**
- Competitive trail runners seeking an edge
- Former road runners transitioning to trails
- Comeback athletes rebuilding after injury
- Coaches managing multiple trail runners

### Competitive Positioning

**Category:** Training Load Intelligence Platform

**We Are:**
- The only platform with normalized divergence analysis
- Trail-specific (elevation-aware calculations)
- Patent-pending technology
- Injury prevention focused

**We Are Not:**
- General fitness tracking (Strava, Garmin Connect)
- Road-running focused (Nike Run Club)
- Generic coaching (TrainingPeaks)
- Wearable hardware company

**Key Differentiator:** We show the relationship between external work and internal response - the divergence that traditional platforms miss.

## Voice & Messaging

### Brand Voice Characteristics

#### Tone Attributes (Scale 1-5)

```
Formal ‚Üê‚Äî‚Äî2‚Äî‚Äî‚Äî‚Äî‚Üí Casual

Respectful ‚Üê‚Äî‚Äî‚Äî‚Äî5‚Äî‚Äî‚Üí Irreverent
```

**Our Sweet Spot:** Professional-friendly with technical credibility

### Voice Guidelines

**DO:**
- Use "we" and "you" to create partnership
- Explain the "why" behind recommendations
- Acknowledge the complexity, then simplify
- Use trail-running terminology authentically
- Lead with benefits, follow with features
- Be direct about risks and limitations
- Celebrate sustainable progress, not just PRs
- Use data to inform, not intimidate

**DON'T:**
- Use corporate jargon or buzzwords
- Over-promise results
- Shame or guilt users
- Use generic fitness platitudes
- Make absolute claims without evidence
- Ignore user context and individuality
- Focus solely on performance gains
- Assume expertise or talk down

### Messaging Framework

#### Tagline
**"Prevent Injuries ‚Äî Train Smarter"**

#### Value Propositions (In Priority Order)

1. **Injury Prevention Through Divergence**
   - "See the training imbalance your Garmin can't show you"
   - "Catch overtraining weeks before injury strikes"
   - "Know when your body and training are out of sync"

2. **Trail-Specific Intelligence**
   - "Built for trail runners, by trail runners"
   - "Vert counts. Our algorithms know it."

3. **Actionable Daily Guidance**
   - "AI coaching recommendations, every single day"
   - "Train with confidence or rest with purpose"
   - "Know exactly what to do today"

4. **Patent-Pending Technology**
   - "Normalized divergence analysis - found nowhere else"
   - "The training status your Garmin can't calculate"
   - "Proprietary algorithm backed by sports science"

### Messaging Do's and Don'ts

#### Headlines

**DO:**
- "The Training Status Your Garmin Can't Show You"
- "See Your Divergence - Prevent Injuries"
- "Trail Running Intelligence for Smart Athletes"

**DON'T:**
- "Revolutionary AI-Powered Training Optimization" (too buzzwordy)
- "Never Get Injured Again!" (over-promise)
- "The Only Training App You'll Ever Need" (absolute claim)

#### Feature Descriptions

**DO:**
- "AI analyzes your acute and chronic workload ratio (ACWR) for both external work and internal stress, then calculates the divergence between them"
- "Our algorithm includes vertical gain in load calculations because trail running isn't flat"
- "Daily coaching recommendations based on your current divergence pattern"

**DON'T:**
- "Machine learning optimizes your training" (vague)
- "Advanced metrics for elite performance" (exclusive/intimidating)
- "AI tells you exactly what to do" (removes agency)

#### Call-to-Action Copy

**Primary CTAs:**
- "Connect with Strava" (specific action)
- "Start Training Smarter" (benefit-focused)
- "See My Divergence" (curiosity-driven)
- "Analyze My Training" (clear outcome)

**Secondary CTAs:**
- "Learn More About Divergence"
- "How It Works"
- "View Tutorial"
- "Explore Features"

### Content Principles

1. **Lead with Pain, Deliver Solution**
   - Start: "Tired of injuries derailing your training?"
   - End: "Your Training Monkey catches the warning signs weeks in advance"

2. **Show, Don't Just Tell**
   - Bad: "Our algorithm is sophisticated"
   - Good: "We analyze 14 different training load metrics and calculate their divergence using a 28-day rolling window"

3. **Use Relatable Scenarios**
   - "You feel great, so you push a hard workout. Three days later, you're inexplicably exhausted. Your Training Monkey would have warned you two days ago that your internal load was diverging dangerfully negative."

4. **Acknowledge Limitations Honestly**
   - "Requires 28+ days of consistent training data for reliable analysis"
   - "Optimized for trail running - road running metrics may be less accurate"
   - "AI recommendations are guidance, not commands - you know your body best"

5. **Celebrate Process Over Outcomes**
   - Focus on "training consistency" not "race PRs"
   - Highlight "injury-free seasons" not "fastest times"
   - Value "sustainable progression" over "breakthrough workouts"

### Form Design

#### Field Layout

**Vertical Stacking** (Default)
- One field per row
- Clear label above field
- Helper text below field
- Best for mobile and accessibility

**Horizontal Grid** (When Appropriate)
- Related fields grouped (Birth Month + Birth Year)
- Maximum 2 columns
- Responsive: Stack on mobile (<768px)

#### Field Sizing

**Content-Appropriate Widths:**
- Numbers (age, year): 120px max
- Email: 100% width
- Select dropdowns: 280px max (or content-dependent)
- Text areas: 100% width

**Never:**
- Make short inputs full-width (looks broken)
- Make long inputs too narrow (causes horizontal scroll)

#### Validation

**Inline Validation:**
- Show errors on blur (when user leaves field)
- Show success checkmark on valid input
- Use color + icon (not color alone for accessibility)
