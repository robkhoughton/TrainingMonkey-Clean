## **THE CORE INNOVATION**

### **Mathematical Foundation**
The Training Monkey Index (TMI) represents a breakthrough four-dimensional parameter combining:
- **Two-dimensional work:load relationship** (external mechanical demands vs. internal physiological response)
- **Two-dimensional acute:chronic relationship** (7-day vs. 28-day temporal patterns)

**Core Formula:**
```
TMI = (external_acwr - internal_acwr) / ((external_acwr + internal_acwr) / 2)
```

Where:
- **External ACWR**: Distance + elevation work ratios (mechanical demands)
- **Internal ACWR**: Heart rate TRIMP load ratios (physiological capacity)

### **The Deeper Purpose: Training the Inner Voice**
Beyond injury prediction, TMI serves as an **objective inner voice** that eliminates the chronic anxiety plaguing performance athletes who obsessively second-guess their training decisions.
